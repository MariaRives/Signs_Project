Traffic Signs Survey

The following survey aims to test drivers' reaction time, comprehensibility, and familiarity of traffic signs.

Before you start the experiment, please open your web browser in full-screen mode following one of the steps below:

1- If you are in a Window desktop computer press 'F11'
2- If you are in a Window laptop press 'Fn + F11'
3- If you are in a macOS computer press 'Command + Control + F'
4- If you are in a macOS computer, alternatively, click the green circle near the top-left corner of the browser window, and choose "Enter Full Screen"
5- Click on the three dots icon located on the top-right corner of the browser window, and select the "Full screen" option from the dropdown menu
